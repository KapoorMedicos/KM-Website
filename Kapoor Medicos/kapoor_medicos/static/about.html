About Kapoor Medicos

Providing quality medicines and healthcare products with trust and care since 1990.

Our Story

Kapoor Medicos was established in 1990 with a mission to provide top-quality pharmaceutical products and healthcare solutions. Over the years, we have built a reputation as a trusted pharmacy, ensuring that our customers receive the best care and guidance.

Our Mission

We strive to make healthcare accessible and convenient for everyone. Our goal is to provide expert guidance, a wide range of medicines, and exceptional customer service to support the well-being of our community.

Why Choose Us?

  • Trusted pharmacy with 30+ years of experience
  • Wide range of high-quality prescription and OTC medicines
  • Expert consultation from professional pharmacists
  • Convenient and reliable service
